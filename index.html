What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is a decentralized digital currency that enables instant transactions and peer-to-peer payments without the need for intermediaries.

Why Bitcoin?

  1. Secure and private transactions
  2. Low transaction fees
  3. Global accessibility
  4. Protection against inflation
